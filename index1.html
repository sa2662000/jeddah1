Jeddah

The city of Jeddah is located in the Lower Hejaz Mountains along the Red Sea west of Saudi Arabia, and is offset on the other side by Sudan, along with Egypt and Eritrea also along the way, while Jordan and Yemen are located north and south of the city along the sea, and the desert represents most of the country's terrain, with coastal plains known as Tihama covering Jeddah.
